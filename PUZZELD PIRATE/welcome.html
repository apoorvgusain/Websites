This a story of a young pirate name JACK who aspire to become the Pirate King.He found a map of a secret treasure which will make him the Pirate King,but the journey is not that easy in order to get the secret treasure our young Pirate have to go through many test.For these test Jack has to be smart enough to solve all the questions.With his team our Young Pirate has started his journey of treasure hunt lets see how far can he go.
